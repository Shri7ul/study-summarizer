ðŸ“˜ Part 3 Summary:
Here is a summary of the lecture content:

*   **Hardware:** These are the computer's physical parts.
    *   **CPU:** The computer's "brain."
    *   **Memory:** Stores information (like RAM, ROM).
    *   **I/O Devices:** Tools to put in or get out information (like mouse, keyboard, screen).
*   **Operating System (OS):** This program helps your programs talk to the hardware and manages the computer.
    *   **OS functions:** Handles basic input/output, gives space for memory, and lets many programs run at once.
    *   **OS examples:** Windows, Linux, MacOS.
*   **Compiler:** This tool changes programs written in human-like languages (like C, Java) into instructions the computer hardware can understand and run.
